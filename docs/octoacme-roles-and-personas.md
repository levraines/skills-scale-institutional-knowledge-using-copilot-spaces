# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Masters

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate effective agile delivery by running ceremonies, coaching the team on working agreements, and removing delivery impediments.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate standups, sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives
- Track and escalate blockers that threaten sprint goals
- Coach teams on agile practices and continuous improvement
- Help maintain healthy team cadence and focus

### Goals
- Improve sprint predictability and flow
- Reduce cycle-time delays caused by unresolved impediments
- Strengthen team collaboration and accountability

### Typical Communication
- Daily blocker follow-ups with Developers and Project Managers
- Sprint ceremony facilitation notes and action items
- Retrospective outcomes shared with Product and Project Managers

### Interaction Points
- Partners with **Project Managers** on timeline risk, dependencies, and escalation
- Coordinates with **Product Managers** on sprint scope clarity
- Supports **Developers** by removing delivery friction and improving team process

---

## UX/UI Designers

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers own the user experience and visual design quality by translating product goals into usable, accessible interfaces.

### Responsibilities
- Create user flows, wireframes, prototypes, and visual specifications
- Validate usability and accessibility before implementation
- Partner with engineering on feasible, testable design solutions
- Maintain consistency through shared design patterns and assets

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user journeys
- Reduce rework caused by unclear or late design decisions
- Improve customer satisfaction through better usability

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Developers and Product Managers
- Specification handoff notes and prototype walkthroughs
- Ongoing implementation feedback during sprint execution

### Interaction Points
- Collaborates with **Product Managers** to align design with customer outcomes
- Works with **Developers** to ensure feasible implementation details
- Aligns with **Project Managers** on design milestones and dependencies

---

## Business Analysts

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge business context and technical delivery by clarifying requirements, documenting processes, and aligning stakeholders.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit, analyze, and document functional and non-functional requirements
- Translate stakeholder needs into clear acceptance-ready requirements
- Map current and target-state workflows
- Identify requirement gaps, assumptions, and cross-team dependencies

### Goals
- Improve requirement clarity before development starts
- Reduce ambiguity-driven rework and handoff issues
- Maintain traceability from business objectives to delivered features

### Typical Communication
- Requirement workshops with stakeholders and Product Managers
- Clarification sessions with Developers and QA/Testing
- Ongoing updates to requirement and workflow documentation

### Interaction Points
- Supports **Product Managers** in refining problem statements and priorities
- Works with **Developers** to ensure implementation-ready requirements
- Partners with **Project Managers** to align scope, risks, and delivery plans

---

## DevOps Engineers

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers manage delivery infrastructure, automation, and operational readiness so teams can ship reliably and safely.

### Responsibilities
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines and deployment workflows
- Manage environments, infrastructure configuration, and observability
- Improve deployment safety through automation and rollback readiness
- Collaborate on reliability, performance, and release operations

### Goals
- Increase deployment reliability and release frequency
- Reduce operational incidents and recovery time
- Improve developer productivity through automation

### Typical Communication
- Release readiness check-ins with Developers and Project Managers
- Deployment runbooks and incident follow-up notes
- Infrastructure change updates shared with delivery stakeholders

### Interaction Points
- Coordinates with **Developers** on build, test, and deployment automation
- Partners with **Project Managers** on release plans and operational risk
- Works with **Security Champions** on secure infrastructure and pipeline controls

---

## Security Champions

### Role Summary
Security Champions advocate for secure development practices within delivery teams and help integrate security into everyday project execution.

### Responsibilities
- Promote secure coding and threat-aware design practices
- Support security reviews of architecture, code, and dependencies
- Help triage and prioritize vulnerability remediation
- Coordinate with central security teams on incidents and policy alignment

### Goals
- Shift security earlier in the delivery lifecycle
- Reduce security defects reaching production
- Improve team awareness of security responsibilities

### Typical Communication
- Security-focused design and PR review feedback
- Risk and vulnerability triage updates during delivery reviews
- Guidance on security requirements for new features and releases

### Interaction Points
- Works with **Developers** on secure implementation and remediation
- Partners with **Project Managers** to track and escalate security risks
- Aligns with **Product Managers** on security requirements and trade-offs

---

## Data Analysts

### Role Summary
Data Analysts define measurement approaches and analyze outcomes so teams can evaluate impact and make evidence-based decisions.

### Responsibilities
- Define success metrics and instrumentation requirements
- Build and maintain dashboards or reports for project outcomes
- Analyze trends, experiment results, and operational signals
- Highlight insights that inform roadmap and delivery decisions

### Goals
- Ensure projects are evaluated against clear success criteria
- Improve decision quality with timely, reliable insights
- Increase visibility into delivery impact and adoption

### Typical Communication
- Metric definition sessions with Product Managers and Business Analysts
- Regular insight updates shared with Project Managers and stakeholders
- Post-release impact reviews with cross-functional teams

### Interaction Points
- Partners with **Product Managers** to connect outcomes to strategy
- Works with **Business Analysts** on measurable requirements and data needs
- Supports **Project Managers** with delivery health and impact reporting

---
